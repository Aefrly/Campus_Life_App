Dining on Campus

We have two facilities to dine at on campus plus our very own Starbucks coffee shop and this page will describe what you can get each day at IC!

Cummings Dining Hall

In the Caine Student Center, we have our cafeteria and this is where you can find most of the students during all meal times. We have our own pizza oven, salad bar, sub maker, grilled foods, home foods, and pantry to select from.

Mondo's Subs

In Baxter Hall, we have our own sub shop where students line up from 11am to 4pm to get custom made subs from some of our favorite workers found on campus.

Starbucks

Finally, we have our own Starbucks shop in Lincoln Hall called Honest Abe's Coffee. We don't have all special Starbucks items, but we do have most regular menu items.
